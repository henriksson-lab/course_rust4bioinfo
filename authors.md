## Acknowledgements

This course material builds on the upstream work of the Rust language team and the bioinformatics-in-Rust community. In particular, the day-4 exercises rely heavily on:

- [`noodles`](https://github.com/zaeleus/noodles) — the pure-Rust HTSlib-style I/O suite
- [`plotters`](https://github.com/plotters-rs/plotters) — the plotting crate
- [`zip`](https://github.com/zip-rs/zip2) — the zip archive crate

Day 5 and day 6 add [`rayon`](https://github.com/rayon-rs/rayon), [`axum`](https://github.com/tokio-rs/axum), and [`yew`](https://github.com/yewstack/yew) to that list. Without them this course would be a third of its length.

## Contributing

The course lives at [henriksson-lab/course_rust4bioinfo](https://github.com/henriksson-lab/course_rust4bioinfo). Corrections, additional exercises, and pull requests are welcome — see the [design principles](designprinciples.md) for the conventions every exercise follows.
